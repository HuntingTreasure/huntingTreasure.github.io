ABOUT US

-

At Hunting Treasure, we are passionate about turning your travel dreams into unforgettable experiences. We were born from a love for adventure and the desire to show the world the hidden treasures of our planet, with a special focus on the magic and history of Cusco, the heart of the Inca Empire.

We believe that traveling goes beyond visiting places; it's about connecting with living cultures, challenging your own limits, and creating stories worth telling. That's why we design each of our itineraries with the care and dedication our travelers deserve, combining the excitement of discovery with the safety and comfort you need.

Our team is made up of expert local guides, passionate storytellers, and nature lovers who know every path, every stone, and every secret corner like the back of their hand. From the imposing citadel of Machu Picchu to the colorful peaks of Vinicunca, through the challenging treks of Choquequirao and the mystical Ausangate, we are here to guide you every step of the way.
